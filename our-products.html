Public Liability Insurance

Public Liability Insurance covers you if a member of the general public is injured or their property is damaged as a result of your activities, regardless of who’s at fault. It is the best way to protect you and your business against the risk, against the things you can’t foresee, so you can get on with those that you can.

Benefits include:

  • Liability cover between $5 million to $20 million

  • Expense cover for rendering first aid to others

  • Legal costs and expenses cover

  • Third party property cover when in your care, custody or control

  • Cover for Products Liability

  • 24 hours claims service

Business Insurance

Business Insurance allows you to protect your business premises and equipment from damage or loss. Covering a broad range of professional occupations.

Your cover options include:

  • Fire and Other Damage Insurance to protect your building and its contents

  • Burglary Insurance against theft of stock or other business equipment

Industrial Special Risk

Industrial Special Risk Insurance is designed for larger, more diverse businesses where it is more practical to combine several individual policies into one. So, if you have assets over $3 million and a diverse number of locations Industrial Special Risk Insurance can be tailored to meet your specific needs under one policy.

Targeted Occupations as examples:

  • Infrastructure (including ports, roads and tunnels)

  • Plastic (injection and extrusion) manufacturers

  • Rubber products manufacturers

  • Wineries

Targeted Properties as examples:

  • Ski lodges and resorts

  • Shopping Centre’s and arcades

  • Backpackers hostels, boarding houses and student accommodation

Corporate Liability Insurance

Corporate Liability provides insurance for most manufacturing risks.

Professional Indemnity Insurance

Professional Indemnity Insurance is designed for people who provide professional advice for a living, such as accountants, marketing consultants, medical professionals and more. It protects you, your employees and your business against claims arising from alleged errors and omissions in carrying out your professional duty.

Such as:

  • when a third party, usually a client, alleges that your professional advice, design or service is negligent and caused them loss – the loss can be bodily injury, property damage or financial loss

  • From a breach of the duty that you owe as a professional or an expert

  • As a Civil Liability from the conduct of your professional services. Coverage includes Contractual liability, liability under the Trade Practices Act, defamation, infringement of intellectual property right, liability for the acts of sub-contractors and consultants, compensation and claimant’s costs and expenses. Other costs such as legal defence costs are payable in addition to the limit of indemnityr

Commercial Vehicle and Motor Trade Insurance

Commercial motor vehicle insurance covers a full range of events from accidental loss or damage, fire, theft or attempted theft, malicious damage or storm damage to your business vehicles. It offers you competitive motor insurance rates. If you are looking for comprehensive commercial motor insurance cover for your passenger car, 4WD, utility, van, prime mover, or trucks over 2 tonnes, we can tailor a policy to suit your needs.

We can arrange two types of commercial motor vehicle insurance cover:

  • Comprehensive cover (Theft or Damage AND Legal Liability)

  • Legal Liability Cover (only)

Talk to us today about how we can tailor commercial motor vehicle insurance that is right for your business or to obtain a quote.

Heavy Motor Vehicle Insurance

Heavy Motor Insurance provides specialised protection for heavy mobile machinery, or vehicles with a carrying capacity over five tons. It is available across a range of occupations including general carriers, tippers and building industry and equipment movers. Transport Package is a flexible insurance product designed for businesses with less than 15 commercial motor items to insure.
It combines four insurance products. You can choose to include all four insurance products in the Transport Package or tailor a package to suit your needs – Commercial Motor is the only mandatory inclusion.

These fourproducts are:

  • Commercial Motor Insurance

  • Liability Covers public and product liability

  • Marine Cargo Cover: The policy offers the choice of Carrier’s Goods In Transit (Perils) Cover or a broader Accidental Damage option.

  • Business Interruption Cover

Contract Works and Legal Liability Insurance

Contract Works and Legal Liability Insurance is designed for businesses who own, run or manage any type of construction site – protecting you against major contingencies before, during and after completion. Covers can be available as a one-off policy for a specific project, or you can take an annual policy covering multiple projects and sites Australia-wide.

This is why Contract Works & Legal Liability Insurance is so important to have if you own, run or manage a construction site. Whether you manage a multi-storey development or an owner builder dwelling, Contract Works & Legal Liability Insurance can provide the right cover to suit your needs.

Liability Insurance provides a safeguard for employers should construction workers become injured on site. Cover also includes materials and machinery on the site in the event of an accident, fire or theft. The high cost of building materials means theft can be a common occurrence and this is a good reason to opt for Contract Works & Legal Liability Insurance.

Construction Insurance and Liability Insurance for both single projects and annual policies on a whole range of risks.
