Claims Support & Assistance

When things go wrong, we’re by your side. At Complete Insurance Advisors, we take the stress off your shoulders and manage the claims process from filing through to settlement.

Dedicated claims support

What We Do for You

  • Lodging your claim and handling paperwork

  • Liaising with insurers on your behalf

  • Monitoring progress and keeping you updated

  • Helping negotiate the best outcome

  • Assisting with appeals or disputes

How to Make a Claim (Step by Step)

  • Notify us: Call or email as soon as possible

  • Provide details: Policy number, contact details, and a description of what happened

  • Gather evidence: Photos, invoices, reports, or witness information if available

  • We submit for you: We lodge the claim with the insurer and keep you updated

  • Resolution: We follow up and help negotiate the best outcome

Do You Need to Make a Claim?

You don’t have to navigate your claim alone. Get in touch and we’ll make the process simple and stress free.
